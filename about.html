Who is KSP?

The story of KS Procurement started in Shanghai — at the center of global trade and manufacturing.

Being deeply exposed to the constant flow of goods in and out of Asia, we began to notice a recurring gap: the disconnect between reliable suppliers and international buyers looking to source from this region.

For many businesses, sourcing from Asia is not just about finding a factory. It comes with language barriers, uncertainty around supplier reliability, and the time-consuming challenge of navigating an unfamiliar production landscape.

Our Role

At KSP, our role is simple — we make sourcing from Asia easier, safer, and more efficient.

Instead of spending months building a local presence or managing suppliers remotely, we act as your bridge on the ground — connecting production directly to your warehouse.

So you can focus on growing your business, while we take care of the part we know best.
